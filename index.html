Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Jodo is a digital wellbeing application designed to help users build healthier relationships with their smartphones. Your privacy matters to us, and this page explains how information is handled within the app.

What is Jodo?

Jodo is a digital wellbeing tool that intervenes at meaningful moments — such as app launches or phone unlocks — to help users become more mindful of their device usage.

Jodo helps users:
  • Understand their phone and app usage patterns
  • Participate in healthy digital wellbeing competitions
  • Improve focus and reduce unnecessary phone usage

Features

  • Interventions at app launch and phone unlock moments
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly leaderboards
  • Competitions focused on mindful phone usage
  • Reward-based motivation system

Data We Collect

Jodo collects limited, non-personal usage data that is essential for providing digital wellbeing insights and app functionality.

The collected data includes:

  • App usage data
  • Phone usage data
  • Number of app launches
  • Number of phone unlocks

Usage Data Sessions

To provide meaningful insights, usage data is organized into the following time sessions:

  • Morning session
  • Evening session
  • Day session
  • Total daily usage summary

How We Use the Data

  • To display usage statistics and insights inside the app
  • To generate leaderboards and competition results
  • To improve app features and user experience

We do not collect personally identifiable information, and we do not sell or share user data with third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your usage data only for as long as necessary to provide the app's features and services:

  • Session data (morning, evening, day, Total sessions) is retained for 90 days from the date of collection.
  • Leaderboard and competition data is retained for the duration of the relevant competition period, plus up to 30 days afterward.
  • Aggregated usage summaries used for app improvement may be retained in anonymised form for up to 12 months.

Once these retention periods expire, your data is securely deleted from our systems. If you delete the Jodo app, your data will be deleted within 30 days of account closure.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your usage data at any time. To submit a data deletion request:

  • Email us at 24m2137@iitb.ac.in with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  • Include any relevant account identifiers (such as your registered email address or username) so we can locate your data.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days. Please note that deleting your data will remove your usage history, leaderboard standings, and competition records, and this action cannot be undone.

Permissions

Jodo may request certain device permissions to track usage statistics. These permissions are used strictly for app functionality and digital wellbeing features.

Children's Privacy

Jodo does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
